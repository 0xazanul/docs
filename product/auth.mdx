## Sign in

- Email and password
- “Remember me” to persist sessions
- “Forgot password?” to reset securely

## Create account

- Full name, email, password, confirm password
- Optionally continue with Google

## Security

- All sessions are protected with modern TLS
- Passwords hashed with strong algorithms (never stored in plaintext)
- Session revocation upon password change
