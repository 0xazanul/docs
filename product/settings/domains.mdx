Key metrics:

- <b>Total Domains</b>, <b>Verified</b>, <b>Pending</b>

## Actions

- <b>Verify domain</b> via DNS TXT record
- <b>Configure</b> domain‑specific scanning rules
- <b>Remove</b> domains you no longer monitor

<Note>
Example states:
- <code>example.com</code> — Verified
- <code>test.com</code> — Verified
- <code>demo.com</code> — Pending verification
</Note>
