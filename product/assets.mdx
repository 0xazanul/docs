## Asset discovery

- Add domains or IP ranges to a group
- Start discovery to enumerate subdomains and services
- Example groups:
  - <b>example.com</b> — 4 subdomains, 37 services — updated 15m ago
  - <b>api.example.com</b> — 2 subdomains, 6 services — updated 3d ago
  - <b>test.com</b> — 5 subdomains, 313 services — updated 28d ago

## Viewing results

- Service list with ports, protocols, first seen, last seen
- Findings list with severity, category, recommended remediation
- Filtering by status, severity, service, and time window
